Storyline

Traitor’s Quest is a game about 4 people of the Cross Kingdom turning on their king, who has caused ruin to their life in one way or another. You can choose to play as the Knight, the Archer, the Thief, or the Wizard, and tear through all those that oppose you from finding freedom through regicide. The game plays like an old SNES game, a bit short, but modernized. The game changes to the character you play as, offering multiple endings depending on which character is the one that felled the final boss, which can give some replayability to the game. It is meant to be a fun experience with a decent story, just like the old days.
